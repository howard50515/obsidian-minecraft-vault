# Nether

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Separate dimension accessed via an [[obsidian]] portal.
* **Requirements:** [[obsidian]] and [[flint_and_steel|Flint and Steel]] to build the portal.
* **Stats/Drops:** Home to [[ancient_debris]], [[nether_quartz_ore]], and hostile mobs like [[ghast|ghasts]].

## The Strategy
The [[nether]] is a high-risk, high-reward environment. Always carry a [[shield]] and a source of light. Prioritize finding a [[nether_fortress|Nether Fortress]] or [[bastion_remnant|Bastion]] for essential materials like [[blaze_rod|Blaze Rods]] and [[netherite]].
