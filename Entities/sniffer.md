# Sniffer

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Does not spawn naturally. Must be hatched from a [[sniffer_egg]], which is found by brushing suspicious sand in warm ocean ruins.
* **Requirements:** [[torchflower_seeds]] for breeding and tempting; [[moss_block]] (optional, reduces hatching time from 20 to 10 minutes).
* **Stats/Drops:** Passive mob. Health: 14 HP (7 hearts). Drops 1–3 XP upon death (if killed by player/tamed wolf). Breeding drops 1–7 XP.

## The Strategy
To obtain a Sniffer, explore warm ocean ruins and use a brush on suspicious sand to find a [[sniffer_egg]]. Place the egg on a [[moss_block]] to halve the hatching time. Once hatched, the "snifflet" takes 40 minutes to grow into an adult, though feeding it [[torchflower_seeds]] reduces growth time by 10% per seed.

Adult Sniffers are used to recover ancient plants. They will occasionally "sploot" and dig up either [[torchflower_seeds]] or a [[pitcher_pod]]. To maximize efficiency, keep them in areas with dirt, grass, or moss, and ensure they are not leashed or in water while digging. You can breed them using [[torchflower_seeds]] to increase your herd size.
