# Sniffer

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Does not spawn naturally. Must be hatched from a [[sniffer_egg]], which is found by brushing [[suspicious_sand]] in [[warm_ocean_ruins|warm ocean ruins]].
* **Requirements:** [[torchflower_seeds]] for breeding and tempting; [[moss_block]] (optional, reduces hatching time from 20 to 10 minutes).
* **Stats/Drops:** Passive mob. Health: 14 HP (7 hearts). Drops 1–3 [[experience_bottle|XP]] upon death (if killed by player/tamed [[wolf]]). Breeding drops 1–7 [[experience_bottle|XP]].

## The Strategy
To obtain a [[sniffer]], explore [[warm_ocean_ruins|warm ocean ruins]] and use a [[brush]] on [[suspicious_sand]] to find a [[sniffer_egg]]. Place the egg on a [[moss_block]] to halve the hatching time. Once hatched, the "snifflet" takes 40 minutes to grow into an adult, though feeding it [[torchflower_seeds]] reduces growth time by 10% per seed.

Adult [[sniffer|Sniffers]] are used to recover ancient plants. They will occasionally "sploot" and dig up either [[torchflower_seeds]] or a [[pitcher_pod]]. To maximize efficiency, keep them in areas with [[dirt]], [[grass_block|grass]], or [[moss_block|moss]], and ensure they are not leashed or in [[water]] while digging. You can breed them using [[torchflower_seeds]] to increase your herd size.
