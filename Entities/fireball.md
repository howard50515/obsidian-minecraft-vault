# Fireball

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Launched by [[ghast]] entities in the [[nether]].
* **Requirements:** None (environmental hazard).
* **Stats/Drops:** Causes explosions upon impact; sets blocks on fire.

## The Strategy
Fireballs can be deflected by punching them back at the [[ghast]] that fired them, resulting in an instant kill if the fireball hits the shooter.

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> Fireballs cause significant blast damage and can destroy blocks or ignite flammable structures. Keep a distance or use a shield.
