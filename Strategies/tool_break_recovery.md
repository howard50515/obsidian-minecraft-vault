# Recovering from Tool Breakage

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Tools break based on durability.
* **Requirements:** Raw materials (e.g., [[cobblestone]], [[iron_ingots]], [[sticks]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** When a tool reaches 0 durability, it disappears.

## The Strategy
If a tool breaks during a task, immediately stop the action to avoid using an inefficient fallback (like mining stone with your fist). Check your inventory for spare materials to craft a temporary replacement. For example, if a pickaxe breaks, use cobblestone to quickly craft a [[stone_pickaxe]] to continue mining.

To prevent this, always carry a "backup kit" containing a few basic tools or the raw materials needed to craft them. In the long term, apply [[mending]] or [[unbreaking]] enchantments to your primary tools to significantly extend their lifespan and reduce the frequency of mid-task breakage.
