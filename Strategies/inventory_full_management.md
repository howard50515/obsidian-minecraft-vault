# Inventory Full Management

## Hard Data
* **Requirements:** [[chest]], [[ender_chest]], [[shulker_box]], [[hopper]]
* **Capacity:** Standard [[inventory]] has ==36== slots (excluding hotbar/armor). [[shulker_box|Shulker Boxes]] provide ==27== slots per box.
* **Stats/Drops:** [[ender_chest]] contents are player-specific across dimensions.

## The Strategy
When the player's [[inventory]] becomes full, efficient management is required to avoid losing valuable resources. The most effective method involves a tiered storage approach, starting with immediate temporary storage and moving toward permanent organization. 

For immediate relief in the field, players should utilize an [[ender_chest]] to store high-value or essential items, as these are accessible from any [[ender_chest]] placed in any dimension. For bulk collection, [[shulker_box|Shulker Boxes]] are the gold standard, allowing the player to carry ==27== slots of items within a single inventory slot. When returning to a base, items should be moved into [[chest|chests]] or automated [[sorting_systems|sorting systems]] using [[hopper|hoppers]] to maintain organization.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use your off-hand slot for a [[torch]] or a [[shulker_box]] to maximize the ==9== hotbar slots for active tools and blocks.

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> Avoid dropping high-value items like [[diamond]] or [[netherite_ingot]] on the ground to make room; items despawn after ==5 minutes== if the chunk remains loaded.

### Recommended Management Steps
- [ ] Identify and drop low-value blocks (e.g., [[cobblestone]], [[dirt]], [[gravel]]).
- [ ] Consolidate items into [[shulker_box|Shulker Boxes]] to save space.
- [ ] Move essential tools and rare materials into an [[ender_chest]].
- [ ] Return to base to offload materials into a [[chest]] or [[hopper|hopper]] system.
- [ ] Optimize the hotbar by placing only the most frequently used items.
