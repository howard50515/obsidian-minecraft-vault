# Surviving the Night Without a Bed

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Hostile mobs spawn in light levels 0.
* **Requirements:** Blocks for shelter (dirt, cobblestone, etc.) or a source of light ([[torch]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** N/A.

## The Strategy
When a [[bed]] is unavailable, the most reliable method is to "hole up." Dig a small 1x2 or 2x2 shelter into a hillside or underground and seal the entrance with a block. This prevents hostile mobs from entering and allows the player to wait out the night in safety.

Alternatively, if you have materials for [[torch]]es, you can create a "safe zone" by lighting up a large area of the ground to prevent mob spawns. If caught in the open, staying in a well-lit area or fighting mobs one-by-one using blocks to create barriers (blocking their path) is the best way to survive until dawn.
