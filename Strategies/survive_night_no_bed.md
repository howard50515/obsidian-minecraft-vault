# Surviving the Night Without a [[bed|Bed]]

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Hostile [[mob|mobs]] spawn in light levels 0.
* **Requirements:** [[block|blocks]] for shelter ([[dirt]], [[cobblestone]], etc.) or a source of light ([[torch]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** N/A.

## The Strategy
When a [[bed]] is unavailable, the most reliable method is to "hole up." Dig a small 1x2 or 2x2 shelter into a hillside or underground and seal the entrance with a [[block]]. This prevents hostile [[mob|mobs]] from entering and allows the player to wait out the night in safety.

Alternatively, if you have materials for [[torch|torches]], you can create a "safe zone" by lighting up a large area of the ground to prevent [[mob|mob spawns]]. If caught in the open, staying in a well-lit area or fighting [[mob|mobs]] one-by-one using [[block|blocks]] to create barriers (blocking their path) is the best way to survive until dawn.
