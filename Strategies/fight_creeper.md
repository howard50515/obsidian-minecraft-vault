# How to Fight a Creeper

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Overworld on solid blocks with light level 0 (except mushroom fields and deep dark).
* **Requirements:** [[shield]] (highly recommended), [[bow]] or [[crossbow]] for range, or a sword for melee.
* **Stats/Drops:** Passive until triggered. Explodes after 1.5s countdown if within 3 blocks. Drops [[gunpowder]].

## The Strategy
The primary goal when fighting a [[creeper]] is to prevent the explosion. The most effective way is using a [[shield]], which can completely block the blast damage. If fighting with a sword, hit the creeper and immediately back away to cancel the detonation timer, which requires the player to move approximately 7 blocks away or break line-of-sight.

For a safer approach, use a [[bow]] or [[crossbow]] to kill it from a distance, as it cannot retaliate with projectiles. If you are in a panic, remember that [[cats]] and ocelots cause creepers to flee, providing a natural defensive perimeter. Avoid fighting them near precious structures, as their explosion destroys blocks.
