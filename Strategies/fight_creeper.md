# How to Fight a [[creeper|Creeper]]

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** [[overworld|Overworld]] on solid [[block|blocks]] with light level 0 (except [[mushroom_fields|mushroom fields]] and [[deep_dark|deep dark]]).
* **Requirements:** [[shield]] (highly recommended), [[bow]] or [[crossbow]] for range, or a [[sword]] for melee.
* **Stats/Drops:** Passive until triggered. Explodes after 1.5s countdown if within 3 [[block|blocks]]. Drops [[gunpowder]].

## The Strategy
The primary goal when fighting a [[creeper]] is to prevent the explosion. The most effective way is using a [[shield]], which can completely block the blast damage. If fighting with a [[sword]], hit the [[creeper]] and immediately back away to cancel the detonation timer, which requires the player to move approximately 7 [[block|blocks]] away or break line-of-sight.

For a safer approach, use a [[bow]] or [[crossbow]] to kill it from a distance, as it cannot retaliate with projectiles. If you are in a panic, remember that [[cat|cats]] and [[ocelot|ocelots]] cause [[creeper|creepers]] to flee, providing a natural defensive perimeter. Avoid fighting them near precious structures, as their explosion destroys [[block|blocks]].
