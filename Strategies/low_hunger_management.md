# Managing Low [[hunger|Hunger]]

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** [[food]] found via [[animal|animals]], [[crop|crops]], or [[village|villages]].
* **Requirements:** Any edible item (e.g., [[cooked_beef]], [[bread]], [[apple]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** [[hunger]] bar must stay above 0 to avoid starvation (on Hard) or [[health]] depletion. Sprinting rapidly depletes [[hunger]].

## The Strategy
When [[hunger]] is low, immediately stop sprinting, as this action rapidly consumes the [[hunger]] bar. Prioritize finding a source of [[food]]; if desperate, kill passive [[mob|mobs]] (like [[pig|pigs]] or [[cow|cows]]) and cook the [[meat]] in a [[furnace]] to maximize the [[hunger_saturation]] provided.

To prevent [[hunger]] from dropping too quickly in the future, maintain a steady diet of high-saturation [[food]]. If you are in a situation where you cannot find [[food]], prioritize walking over sprinting and avoid taking damage, as [[hunger]] is required for natural [[health]] regeneration.
