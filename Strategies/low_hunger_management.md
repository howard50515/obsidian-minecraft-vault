# Managing Low Hunger

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Food found via animals, crops, or villages.
* **Requirements:** Any edible item (e.g., [[cooked_beef]], [[bread]], [[apple]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** Hunger bar must stay above 0 to avoid starvation (on Hard) or health depletion. Sprinting rapidly depletes hunger.

## The Strategy
When hunger is low, immediately stop sprinting, as this action rapidly consumes the hunger bar. Prioritize finding a source of food; if desperate, kill passive mobs (like pigs or cows) and cook the meat in a [[furnace]] to maximize the hunger saturation provided.

To prevent hunger from dropping too quickly in the future, maintain a steady diet of high-saturation foods. If you are in a situation where you cannot find food, prioritize walking over sprinting and avoid taking damage, as hunger is required for natural health regeneration.
