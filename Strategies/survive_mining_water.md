# Surviving Sudden [[water|Water]] While Mining

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Occurs when mining into an underground [[water]] pocket or [[ocean_cave|ocean cave]].
* **Requirements:** [[torch]] or [[block|blocks]] for plugging holes.
* **Stats/Drops:** [[water]] causes slow movement and prevents breathing ([[drowning]]).

## The Strategy
If you break a [[block]] and [[water]] suddenly floods your tunnel, the immediate priority is to stop the flow. Place a [[block]] ([[dirt]], [[cobblestone]]) directly into the hole you just created to seal the leak. This prevents the [[water]] from filling your mining area and potentially [[drowning]] you.

If you are already submerged, swim upwards to find an air pocket or dig a path to the surface. Avoid panic-mining in all directions, as you might open more [[water]] pockets. Once safe, use a [[bucket]] to remove excess [[water]] or plant a [[torch]] nearby to identify the source of the leak more clearly.
