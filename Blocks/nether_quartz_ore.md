# Nether Quartz Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any Nether biome. Most found at layer 114.
* **Requirements:** [[wooden_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops quartz shards.

## The Strategy
Quartz is essential for observers and various decorative blocks. It is very common in the Nether, making it an easy source of experience points for early-game players.

Since it can be mined with any pickaxe, you don't need high-tier gear to gather it. However, be careful of the surrounding environment (lava and ghasts) while mining.
