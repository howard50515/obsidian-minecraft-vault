# Emerald Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Only in mountains and windswept hills biomes. Total range: -16 to 320. Most found at layer 85.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops 1 [[emerald]].

## The Strategy
Emerald ore is the rarest ore per chunk. The best way to find it is to explore high-altitude mountain biomes. 

While emeralds are more easily obtained by trading with villagers, mining them is a challenge for completionists. Use [[fortune]] to increase the chance of getting more emeralds from a single block.
