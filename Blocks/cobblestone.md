# Cobblestone

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Obtained by mining [[stone]] with any [[pickaxe]]. Generated in large quantities in the [[overworld]] and in various structures.
* **Requirements:** Any [[pickaxe]] (e.g., [[wooden_pickaxe]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** Used as a primary building material and for crafting [[stone_pickaxe]]s and [[furnace]]s.

## The Strategy
[[cobblestone|Cobblestone]] is the most basic building [[block]] in the game. Once you mine your first few blocks of [[stone]], you can use them to upgrade from [[wooden_tool|wooden tools]] to [[stone_tool|stone tools]]. It can be smelted into [[stone]] or used to craft [[furnace]]s.
