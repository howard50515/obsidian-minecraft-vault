# Crafting Table

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using ==4== [[planks]]. Also found in [[village]] structures.
* **Requirements:** None (can be broken by hand).
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops itself as an item.

## The Strategy
The [[crafting_table]] is the most fundamental utility [[block]] in [[minecraft]]. It expands the player's crafting capability from a ==2x2== grid to a ==3x3== grid, enabling the creation of almost all tools, armor, and complex machinery.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Place your [[crafting_table]] in a central location of your base to minimize travel time when crafting.
