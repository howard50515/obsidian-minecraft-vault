# Crop

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Grown on [[farmland]] using various seeds (e.g., [[wheat]], [[carrot]], [[potato]]).
* **Requirements:** [[hoe]] to prepare [[farmland]], water source for hydration.
* **Stats/Drops:** Varies by crop; typically drops the harvestable item and some seeds.

## The Strategy
Crops are the primary source of renewable food and materials. To maximize yield, plant crops in rows of two with a water block in the center; a single water source can hydrate farmland up to ==4== blocks away in any direction.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use [[fortune]] enchanted tools on certain crops or use [[bone_meal]] to instantly grow crops to their final stage.
