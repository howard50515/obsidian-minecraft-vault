# Water

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found in oceans, rivers, and underground lakes throughout the [[overworld]].
* **Requirements:** [[bucket]] to collect.
* **Stats/Drops:** Put out fire, neutralizes [[lava]], and flows downward.

## The Strategy
[[water]] is an essential survival tool. It is used to create [[obsidian]] by pouring it over [[lava]] and to prevent death from falling by creating a "water landing."

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Keep a [[bucket]] of [[water]] on your hotbar at all times when exploring [[cave|caves]] to quickly extinguish fires or save yourself from a fall.
