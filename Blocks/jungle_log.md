# Jungle Log

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found in [[overworld]] jungle biomes.
* **Requirements:** Any [[axe]] is fastest; can be broken by hand.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops ==1== [[jungle_log]] when mined.

## The Strategy
[[jungle_log|Jungle logs]] come from some of the tallest trees in the game. To harvest them efficiently, bring a stack of [[dirt]] or [[cobblestone]] to build a temporary pillar to reach the canopy. Be mindful of [[monkey|monkeys]] (if mods are present) or just the dense foliage that can obscure your vision.
