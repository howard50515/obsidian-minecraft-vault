# Obsidian

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Formed when [[water]] contacts a [[lava]] source [[block]]. Found at the bottom of [[lava]] lakes.
* **Requirements:** Must be mined with a [[diamond_pickaxe]] or [[netherite_pickaxe]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Extremely high blast resistance. Required for constructing a [[nether_portal|Nether portal]].

## The Strategy
To mine [[obsidian]], ensure you have a [[diamond_tier_tool|diamond-tier tool]]; anything else will break the [[block]] without dropping it. Use a [[bucket]] of [[water]] to create [[obsidian]] from [[lava]] safely. 

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> Do not mine [[obsidian]] over [[lava]] without a safe platform, as falling into [[lava]] while mining can be fatal.
