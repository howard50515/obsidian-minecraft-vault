# Terracotta

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found abundantly in [[badlands.md|Badlands]] biomes. Can also be created by smelting [[clay]] blocks.
* **Requirements:** Can be mined with any pickaxe, but a [[diamond_pickaxe]] or [[iron_pickaxe]] is standard for efficiency.
* **Stats/Drops:** Comes in various colors; primarily used as a decorative building block.

## The Strategy
Terracotta is ideal for large-scale construction due to its variety of colors. In the [[badlands.md|Badlands]], you can find massive veins of colored terracotta naturally.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use a pickaxe with [[silk_touch]] to ensure you collect the specific variant of terracotta you are mining without it breaking into a generic form (if applicable to certain variants).
