## The Strategy
[[stone_bricks]] turn a rough castle into a refined fortress. They are the primary material for "medieval" architecture. Variants include [[mossy_stone_bricks]], [[cracked_stone_bricks]], and [[chiseled_stone_bricks]], which can be used to add age and detail to a build.
