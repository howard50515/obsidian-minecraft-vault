# Blast Furnace

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using one [[furnace]], ==5== [[iron_ingot]]s, and ==3== [[smooth_stone]] blocks.
* **Requirements:** [[crafting_table|Crafting table]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Smelts [[ore|ores]] and metal items ==2x== faster than a standard [[furnace]].

## The Strategy
Use the [[blast_furnace]] specifically for [[ore|ores]] and metal armor/tools. It cannot cook food, so keep a regular [[furnace]] nearby for that. It is an excellent way to process large amounts of [[iron]] or [[gold]] quickly.
