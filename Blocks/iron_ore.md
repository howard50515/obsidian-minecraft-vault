# Iron Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any [[biome]]. Total range: ==-64 to 32==. Most found at layer ==14==.
* **Requirements:** [[stone_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops ==1== [[raw_iron|raw iron]].

## The Strategy
[[iron|Iron]] is the primary material for mid-game tools and armor. Focus your search in [[mountains]] or underground [[cave|caves]]. In the new world generation, iron is very abundant at higher altitudes and at layer ==14==.

Since [[iron_ore|iron ore]] drops "raw iron," you must use a [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]] to smelt it into [[iron_ingot]]s. 

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Using a [[fortune]] [[pickaxe]] will increase the amount of [[raw_iron|raw iron]] dropped.
