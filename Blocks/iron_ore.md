# Iron Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any biome. Total range: -64 to 32. Most found at layer 14.
* **Requirements:** [[stone_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops 1 raw iron.

## The Strategy
Iron is the primary material for mid-game tools and armor. Focus your search in mountains or underground caves. In the new world generation, iron is very abundant at higher altitudes (mountains) and at layer 14.

Since iron ore drops "raw iron," you must use a [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]] to smelt it into iron ingots. Using a [[fortune]] pickaxe will increase the amount of raw iron dropped.
