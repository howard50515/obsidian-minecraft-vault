# Stone

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found throughout the [[overworld]], making up the majority of underground blocks above ==Y=0==.
* **Requirements:** Any [[pickaxe]] to mine; drops [[cobblestone]] unless mined with [[silk_touch]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops ==1== [[cobblestone]] by default.

## The Strategy
Stone is the most abundant block in the game. It is the primary source for creating [[cobblestone]], which is essential for early-game tools and structures. To obtain the stone block itself, use a [[pickaxe]] with [[silk_touch]] or smelt [[cobblestone]] in a [[furnace]].
