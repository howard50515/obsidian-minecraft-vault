# Diamond Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any biome. Total range: -64 to 16. Most found at layer -59.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops 1 [[diamond]].

## The Strategy
Diamonds are the most coveted resource for high-tier gear. The optimal mining depth is Y=-59. When you find a vein, clear the surrounding blocks to ensure you've collected all connected diamonds.

It is highly recommended to use a pickaxe with [[fortune]] to get more diamonds per block. If you need the ore block itself for decoration, use [[silk_touch]].
