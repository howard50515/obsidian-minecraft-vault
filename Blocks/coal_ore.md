# Coal Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any [[biome]]. Total range: ==0 to 320==. Most found at layer ==45==.
* **Requirements:** [[wooden_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops ==1== [[coal]].

## The Strategy
[[coal|Coal]] is the most common fuel source and essential for early-game smelting. Since it spawns at almost any altitude, it is often found while digging a basic starter base or exploring surface [[cave|caves]]. 

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use a [[pickaxe]] with [[fortune]] to significantly increase the number of [[coal]] pieces dropped per block. 

If you are in the [[deepslate]] layers, look for the [[deepslate_coal_ore|deepslate variant of coal ore]].
