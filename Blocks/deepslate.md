# Deepslate

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found in the [[overworld]], primarily at deep elevations (below ==Y=0==).
* **Requirements:** Any [[pickaxe]] to mine; drops [[cobbled_deepslate]] unless mined with [[silk_touch]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops ==1== [[cobbled_deepslate]] by default.

## The Strategy
[[deepslate]] is harder than regular stone, taking longer to mine. It is the primary material for late-game underground bases. Because it is found so deep, be prepared for increased mob spawns and the risk of encountering the [[warden]] in deep dark biomes.
