# Dark Oak Log

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found in [[overworld]] roofed forests.

## The Strategy
[[dark_oak_log|Dark oak logs]] typically grow in 2x2 trunks. This means you must harvest ==4== base blocks to fully clear a tree. Their deep brown color is one of the best for creating heavy-duty framing or flooring.
