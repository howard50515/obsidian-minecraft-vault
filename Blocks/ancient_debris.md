# Ancient Debris

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** The Nether. Most found at layer 16.
* **Requirements:** [[diamond_pickaxe]] or [[netherite_pickaxe]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops itself. Must be smelted to obtain [[netherite_scrap]].

## The Strategy
Ancient Debris is the rarest material in the game. The most effective way to find it is "bed mining" or "TNT mining" at Y=15 or Y=16 in the Nether, as it is blast-resistant and will not be destroyed by explosions.

Once you find it, mine it with a diamond or netherite pickaxe, smelt it in a [[furnace]], and combine the resulting scrap with gold to create netherite ingots for the highest tier of gear.
