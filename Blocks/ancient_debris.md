# Ancient Debris

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** The [[nether]]. Most found at layer ==16==.
* **Requirements:** [[diamond_pickaxe]] or [[netherite_pickaxe]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops itself. Must be smelted to obtain [[netherite_scrap]].

## The Strategy
[[ancient_debris|Ancient Debris]] is the rarest material in the game. 

- [ ] Descent to ==Y-level 15== or ==16== in the [[nether]].
- [ ] Use "bed mining" or "[[tnt|TNT]] mining" to blast large areas (debris is blast-resistant).
- [ ] Mine the exposed debris with a [[diamond_pickaxe]] or [[netherite_pickaxe]].
- [ ] Smelt the debris in a [[furnace]] to get [[netherite_scrap]].
- [ ] Combine [[netherite_scrap|scrap]] with [[gold_ingot]]s to create [[netherite_ingot]]s.

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> When using the "bed mining" technique, ensure you are safely tucked into a 1x2 tunnel to avoid being blown up by your own [[bed]] or falling into unexpected [[lava]] pockets.
