# Redstone Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any biome. Total range: -64 to 16. Most found at layer -59.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops 4–5 redstone dust.

## The Strategy
Redstone is the foundation of all automation and circuitry in Minecraft. It generates in the same deep layers as diamonds. 

Using a [[fortune]] pickaxe is extremely effective here, as it significantly increases the amount of redstone dust dropped. Since it is often found in large veins, it is a great source of experience and material for redstone components.
