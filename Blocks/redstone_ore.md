## The Strategy
[[redstone|Redstone]] is the foundation of all automation and circuitry in [[minecraft]]. It generates in the same deep layers as [[diamond|diamonds]]. 

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Using a [[fortune]] [[pickaxe]] is extremely effective here, as it significantly increases the amount of [[redstone_dust|redstone dust]] dropped. 

Since it is often found in large veins, it is a great source of [[experience|experience]] and material for [[redstone]] components.
