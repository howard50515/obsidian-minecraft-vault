# Ores

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Various altitudes across the [[overworld]] and the [[nether]].
* **Requirements:** Specific tiered [[pickaxe]]es depending on the [[ore]] type.
* **Stats/Drops:** Resources used for crafting tools, armor, and [[redstone]] circuits.

## The Strategy
[[ore|Ores]] are the primary drivers of progression in [[minecraft]]. To maximize efficiency, players should identify the "optimal layer" for the specific resource they are seeking (e.g., ==Y-level -59== for [[diamond_ore]]). 

Using a [[pickaxe]] with [[fortune]] is highly recommended for [[ore|ores]] that drop multiple units (like [[redstone_ore]] or [[lapis_lazuli_ore]]), while [[silk_touch]] is used to collect the [[ore]] block itself.
