# Ores

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Various altitudes across the Overworld and the Nether.
* **Requirements:** Specific tiered [[pickaxe]]es depending on the ore type.
* **Stats/Drops:** Resources used for crafting tools, armor, and redstone circuits.

## The Strategy
Ores are the primary drivers of progression in Minecraft. To maximize efficiency, players should identify the "optimal layer" for the specific resource they are seeking (e.g., Y=-59 for diamonds). Using a pickaxe with [[fortune]] is highly recommended for ores that drop multiple units (like redstone or lapis), while [[silk_touch]] is used to collect the ore block itself.
