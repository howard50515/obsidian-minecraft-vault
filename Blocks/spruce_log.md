# Spruce Log

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found in [[overworld]] taiga and snowy biomes.
* **Requirements:** Any [[axe]] is fastest; can be broken by hand.

## The Strategy
[[spruce_log|Spruce logs]] are often found in "mega" spruce trees, which provide a massive amount of wood in a single location. Their dark, muted tone is highly preferred for medieval-style builds. Since they spawn in colder biomes, keep a [[torch]] handy to light up the area while harvesting.
