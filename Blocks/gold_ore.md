# Gold Ore

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Any biome, but increased frequency in [[badlands]]. Total range: -64 to 256. Most found at layer -18.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops 1 raw gold.

## The Strategy
Gold is essential for crafting powered rails and bartering with piglins. If you are searching for gold, the [[badlands]] biome is the most efficient location due to its increased spawn rate.

Like iron, gold ore must be smelted in a [[furnace]] to create gold ingots. Use a [[fortune]] pickaxe to maximize the yield of raw gold.
