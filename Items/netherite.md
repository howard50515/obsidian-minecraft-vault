# Netherite

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found as [[ancient_debris]] in the [[nether|Nether]], which is smelted into [[netherite_scrap]]. Four scraps and four [[gold_ingots|gold ingots]] are combined to make a [[netherite_ingot]].
* **Requirements:** Requires a [[diamond_pickaxe]] to mine the source ore ([[ancient_debris]]).
* **Stats/Drops:** Immune to lava and fire. Used to upgrade [[diamond]] gear using a [[netherite_upgrade_smithing_template]].

## The Strategy
Netherite is the strongest material in the game. To obtain it, travel to the [[nether|Nether]] and mine at low Y-levels (typically ==Y-level 15==). Because [[ancient_debris]] is rare and blast-resistant, "strip mining" or using [[tnt]] is the most effective method.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Since [[netherite_ingot|Netherite Ingots]] do not burn in lava, you don't need to worry about losing them if you accidentally fall into a lava lake while mining.
