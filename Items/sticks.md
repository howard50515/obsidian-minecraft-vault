# Sticks

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted from [[planks]] (two [[planks]] stacked vertically) or obtained as junk while fishing.

## The Strategy
[[sticks]] are a fundamental crafting ingredient. Always keep a stack of [[sticks]] in your inventory when crafting tools like [[pickaxe]]s, axes, or shovels. They are also used to craft [[torch]]es when combined with [[coal]] or [[charcoal]].
