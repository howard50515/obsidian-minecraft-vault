# Flint and Steel

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted by combining ==1x [[iron_ingot]]== and ==1x [[flint]]== in a crafting grid.
* **Requirements:** No specific tool required to use, but requires an [[iron_ingot]] and [[flint]] for creation.
* **Stats/Drops:** Used to ignite [[lava]], fire, or specific blocks like [[obsidian]] (when creating portals).

## The Strategy
The [[flint_and_steel|Flint and Steel]] is an essential utility tool. Its primary use is to light fires or ignite a portal frame. When used on a flammable block, it creates a fire block.

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> Be extremely careful when using this tool near flammable structures (like wooden houses) or in the [[overworld|Overworld]] forests, as fire can spread rapidly and destroy your base.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use a [[flint_and_steel|Flint and Steel]] to burn away large areas of grass or leaves quickly to clear land for building.
