# Emerald

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Dropped by mining [[emerald_ore]] or [[deepslate_emerald_ore]]. Also obtained via trading with [[villager]]s.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better to mine the ore.
* **Stats/Drops:** Primary currency for trading with [[villager]]s and [[wandering_trader]]s.

## The Strategy
While mining [[emeralds]] is possible in [[mountains]] and [[windswept_hills]], trading with [[villager]]s is significantly more efficient for accumulating [[emeralds]]. When mining, use [[fortune]] to increase the drops.
