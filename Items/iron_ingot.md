# Iron Ingot

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Smelted from [[raw_iron]] or [[iron_ore]].
* **Requirements:** [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]] for smelting.
* **Stats/Drops:** Fundamental material for tools, armor, and [[redstone]] components.

## The Strategy
Iron is the backbone of early-game progression. Focus on mining iron in [[mountains]] or underground caves. Using a [[fortune]] [[pickaxe]] on [[iron_ore]] increases the [[raw_iron]] yield, which can then be smelted into [[iron_ingots]].
