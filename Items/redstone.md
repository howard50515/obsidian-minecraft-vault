# Redstone

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Obtained by mining [[redstone_ore]] or deepslate redstone ore.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Base material for redstone components and dust.

## The Strategy
[[redstone]] is the core material for all automation. Whether you are building a simple auto-door or a complex computer, you will need a steady supply of redstone.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Redstone generates in the same deep layers as [[diamond_ore]]. While mining for diamonds, keep an eye out for redstone veins.
