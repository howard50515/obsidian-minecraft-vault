# Iron Ingots

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Smelted from [[raw_iron]] or [[iron_ore]].
* **Requirements:** [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Core material for [[iron_pickaxe]], [[bucket]]s, and armor.

## The Strategy
Iron is the first "true" progression metal. Prioritize gathering iron early to create a [[bucket]] (for [[lava]]/[[water]]) and an [[iron_pickaxe]], which allows you to mine [[diamonds]].
