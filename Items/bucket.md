## The Strategy
The [[bucket]] is a critical utility tool. It allows the player to transport liquids across the [[world]].

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> When placing [[lava]] from a [[bucket]], ensure you are not standing on flammable blocks or near your own base to avoid catastrophic fire.

Use a [[bucket]] of [[water]] to instantly neutralize [[lava]] and create [[obsidian]] or [[cobblestone]].
