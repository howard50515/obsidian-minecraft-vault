# Copper Pickaxe

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using two [[sticks]] and three [[copper_ingots]].
* **Requirements:** A crafting table.
* **Stats/Drops:** Durability (191) and speed sit between stone and iron. Mining level is functionally identical to a stone pickaxe (mining level 1).

## The Strategy
A niche tool introduced in recent versions. While it has better durability than stone, it does not allow you to mine blocks that a [[stone_pickaxe]] couldn't. Use it if you have an abundance of copper and lack stone.
