# Iron Pickaxe

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using two [[sticks]] and three [[iron_ingots]]. Found in [[mineshaft]] chests, [[stronghold]] chests, and [[village]] Toolsmith/Weaponsmith chests.
* **Requirements:** A crafting table.
* **Stats/Drops:** High durability (251) and speed. Required for mining gold, diamond, and redstone ores.

## The Strategy
The most important mid-game tool. Once you have an iron pickaxe, you can access the most valuable materials in the game. Prioritize getting this immediately after the stone tier to begin your search for [[diamond_ore]].
