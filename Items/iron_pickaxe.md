* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using two [[sticks]] and three [[iron_ingots]]. Found in [[mineshaft]] chests, [[stronghold]] chests, and [[village]] [[toolsmith]]/[[weaponsmith]] chests.
* **Requirements:** A [[crafting_table]].

The most important mid-game tool. Once you have an [[iron_pickaxe]], you can access the most valuable materials in the game. Prioritize getting this immediately after the stone tier to begin your search for [[diamond_ore]].
