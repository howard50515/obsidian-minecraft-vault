# Raw Gold

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Obtained by mining [[gold_ore]] or deepslate gold ore with an [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Smelted into [[gold_ingot]] using a [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]].

## The Strategy
[[raw_gold]] is the raw form of gold. It is primarily used for smelting into [[gold_ingot]]s, which are essential for [[powered_rail]]s and bartering with [[piglin|piglins]] in the [[nether]].

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Using a [[pickaxe]] with [[fortune]] significantly increases the yield of [[raw_gold]] when mining gold ore.
