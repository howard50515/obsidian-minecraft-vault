# Experience Bottle

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Dropped by some mobs, found in loot chests, or traded from [[villagers|Cleric villagers]].
* **Requirements:** None to use; just break it on the ground.
* **Stats/Drops:** Drops ==3–11== experience points per bottle.

## The Strategy
Experience bottles (Bottle o' Enchanting) allow players to gain [[experience_bottle|XP]] rapidly without killing mobs. They are essential for high-level enchanting and repairing [[mending]] gear.
