# Diamond

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Dropped by mining [[diamond_ore]] or [[deepslate_diamond_ore]]. Found in loot chests in various structures.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better to mine the ore.
* **Stats/Drops:** Used to craft high-tier tools, armor, and [[enchanting_table]]s.

## The Strategy
Diamonds are critical for mid-to-late game progression. Mine at ==Y-level -59== for the highest concentration of [[diamond_ore]]. Use a [[fortune]] enchanted [[pickaxe]] to increase the yield of [[diamonds]] per block, and [[silk_touch]] if you wish to transport the ore blocks to a more secure location before mining them.
