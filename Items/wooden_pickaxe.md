# Wooden Pickaxe

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using two [[sticks]] and three [[planks]] (of any wood type). Can be found in [[bonus_chest]].
* **Requirements:** A [[crafting_table]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Lowest tier durability (59) and mining speed. Can mine [[stone]] and [[cobblestone]], but cannot harvest ores like [[iron_ore]] or above.

## The Strategy
The [[wooden_pickaxe]] is a "stepping stone" tool. Its only real purpose is to mine enough [[stone]] to immediately craft a [[stone_pickaxe]]. Do not waste time upgrading other tools to wood; get to stone as fast as possible to increase efficiency.
