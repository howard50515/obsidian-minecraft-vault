# Gold Ingot

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Smelted from [[raw_gold]], [[gold_ore]], or [[nether_gold_ore]].
* **Requirements:** [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]] for smelting.
* **Stats/Drops:** Used for crafting, [[powered_rail]]s, and bartering with [[piglin]]s.

## The Strategy
Gold is most abundant in the [[badlands]] biome. Use [[gold_ingots]] for bartering with [[piglin]]s in the [[nether]] to get rare materials, or use them to craft essential rail systems for transport.
