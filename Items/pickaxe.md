# Pickaxe

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using two [[sticks]] and three units of a tier's material.
* **Requirements:** A [[crafting_table]] for most variants; a [[smithing_table]] for [[netherite]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Tier-based durability and mining speed. Higher tiers allow harvesting of rarer blocks (e.g., [[diamond_pickaxe]] is required for [[obsidian]]).

## The Strategy
The [[pickaxe]] is the most critical tool for progression. Always start by crafting a [[wooden_pickaxe]] to mine [[cobblestone]], then immediately upgrade to a [[stone_pickaxe]] to increase efficiency and durability. Progress rapidly to an [[iron_pickaxe]] to enable the mining of [[gold_ore]], [[diamond_ore]], and [[redstone_ore]].

To maximize the lifespan of your tools, prioritize obtaining the [[mending]] and [[unbreaking]] enchantments. If you are mining for high-value ores, use a [[pickaxe]] with [[fortune]] to increase the drop yield, or [[silk_touch]] if you need to collect the block itself.
