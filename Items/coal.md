# Coal

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Dropped by mining [[coal_ore]] or [[deepslate_coal_ore]]. Also found in loot chests.
* **Requirements:** [[wooden_pickaxe]] or better to mine the ore.
* **Stats/Drops:** Used as a primary fuel source for [[furnace]] and [[blast_furnace]].

## The Strategy
Coal is the most fundamental fuel in the early game. To maximize your supply, always use a [[pickaxe]] with [[fortune]] when mining [[coal_ore]], as it significantly increases the number of [[coal]] pieces dropped per block.
