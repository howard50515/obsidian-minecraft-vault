# Stone Pickaxe

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Crafted using two [[sticks]] and three units of [[cobblestone]], [[stone]], [[blackstone]], or [[cobbled_deepslate]]. Found in [[bonus_chest]].
* **Requirements:** A [[crafting_table]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Moderate durability (131) and speed. Can mine [[iron_ore]], [[copper_ore]], and [[coal_ore]].

## The Strategy
The primary early-game tool. Use this to gather the [[iron_ingots]] needed for an [[iron_pickaxe]]. It is also useful to keep a spare in your inventory for emergency recovery if your higher-tier tools break while mining deep.
