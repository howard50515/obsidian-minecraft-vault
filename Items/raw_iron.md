# Raw Iron

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Obtained by mining [[iron_ore]] or deepslate iron ore with a [[stone_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Requirements:** [[stone_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Smelted into [[iron_ingot]] using a [[furnace]] or [[blast_furnace]].

## The Strategy
[[raw_iron]] is the intermediate state of iron. To make it useful, you must smelt it.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use a [[blast_furnace]] to smelt [[raw_iron]] ==2x== faster than a regular [[furnace]].
