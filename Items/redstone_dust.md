# Redstone Dust

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Obtained by mining [[redstone_ore]] or deepslate redstone ore.
* **Requirements:** [[iron_pickaxe]] or better.
* **Stats/Drops:** Used to transmit redstone power between components.

## The Strategy
[[redstone_dust]] is the "wire" of the Minecraft world. It is used to connect power sources (like [[lever]]s or [[redstone_torch]]s) to mechanisms (like pistons or [[tnt]]).

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use a [[pickaxe]] with [[fortune]] to maximize the amount of [[redstone_dust]] obtained from a single [[redstone_ore]] block.
