# Unbreaking

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Available via [[enchanting_table]] or trading with [[villagers]].
* **Requirements:** [[enchanting_table]], [[lapis_lazuli]].
* **Stats/Drops:** Reduces the chance that a tool's durability will decrease when used.

## The Strategy
Combine [[unbreaking]] with [[mending]] to maximize the lifespan of your tools. [[unbreaking]] III is highly recommended for any tool you intend to use frequently, as it significantly slows down the rate of wear and tear.
