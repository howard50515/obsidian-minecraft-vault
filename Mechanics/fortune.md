# Fortune

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Obtained via an [[enchanting_table]] or as loot from raid mobs.
* **Requirements:** An [[enchanting_table]] and [[experience]] levels.
* **Stats/Drops:** Increases the number of item drops from certain [[block|blocks]] (e.g., [[ore|ores]]).

## The Strategy
The [[fortune]] enchantment is essential for resource gathering. Apply it to your [[pickaxe]] to drastically increase the yield of [[coal]], [[diamond]], [[emerald]], [[lapis_lazuli_ore]], and [[redstone_ore]]. This reduces the amount of mining required to gather large quantities of materials.
