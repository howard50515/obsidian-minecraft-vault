# Mending

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Found as a treasure enchantment in loot [[chest|chests]] or traded by [[villagers]].
* **Requirements:** [[anvil]] or [[enchanting_table]] (via trading).
* **Stats/Drops:** Restores tool/armor durability using [[experience]] (XP) points collected by the player.

## The Strategy
[[mending]] is the most powerful durability enchantment. Prioritize putting it on your [[netherite_pickaxe]] and armor. Once applied, your gear will effectively never break as long as you continue to gain [[experience|XP]].
