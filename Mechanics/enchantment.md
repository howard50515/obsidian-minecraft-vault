# Enchantment

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Applied via [[enchanting_table]], [[anvil]], or found on loot items.
* **Requirements:** [[experience_bottle|XP]], [[lapis_lazuli]], and a compatible item.
* **Stats/Drops:** Modifies item stats (damage, durability, special abilities).

## The Strategy
Enchantments are critical for late-game survival. Use an [[enchanting_table]] for random rolls, but use an [[anvil]] with enchanted books for precise control.

> [!tip] Efficiency Tip
> Use [[mending]] on your most valuable tools to repair them using [[experience_bottle|XP]] instead of materials.
