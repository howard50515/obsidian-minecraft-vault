# Warm Ocean Ruins

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Generates in warm, lukewarm, and deep lukewarm ocean biomes.
* **Requirements:** Underwater exploration.
* **Stats/Drops:** Contains chests with loot and [[suspicious_sand]].

## The Strategy
Warm ocean ruins are composed primarily of sandstone. They are excellent sources of [[sniffer]] eggs, which can be found by brushing [[suspicious_sand]].

> [!danger] CRITICAL WARNING
> Ruins are often inhabited by [[drowned]], some of whom carry tridents. Always carry a weapon and a way to breathe underwater.
