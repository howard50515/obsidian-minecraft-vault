# Mineshaft

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Underground structures consisting of [[tunnel]]s and [[support]]s.
* **Requirements:** [[torch]]es and a [[pickaxe]].
* **Stats/Drops:** [[chest]]s containing [[rail]]s, [[iron]], and [[gold]]. Spawns [[cave_spider]]s.

## The Strategy
Follow the [[wooden_support]]s to explore mineshafts. They are excellent for finding [[iron]] and [[gold]] quickly. Be wary of [[cobweb]]s and [[cave_spider]]s; use a [[sword]] or [[axe]] to clear the path.
