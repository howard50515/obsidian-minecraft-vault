# Bonus Chest

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Spawns near the [[player]] if "Bonus Chest" is enabled in world settings.
* **Requirements:** None.
* **Stats/Drops:** Contains basic survival [[items]], [[food]], and occasionally [[tools]] like a [[wooden_pickaxe]].

## The Strategy
Check the [[bonus_chest]] immediately upon spawning to get a head start on your resources. It often provides enough [[food]] and basic materials to skip the first few minutes of grinding.
