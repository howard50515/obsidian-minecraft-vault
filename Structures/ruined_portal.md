# Ruined Portal

## Hard Data
* **Spawns/Found/Crafted:** Naturally generated structures in the [[overworld]] and [[nether]].
* **Requirements:** None to find; [[obsidian]]/[[flint_and_steel]] to complete.
* **Stats/Drops:** [[chest]]s often contain [[obsidian]], [[flint]], and [[fire_charge]]s.

## The Strategy
Ruined portals are great early-game sources of [[obsidian]]. Use the materials in the associated [[chest]] to complete the [[portal]] and gain access to the [[nether]] without having to mine all the [[obsidian]] yourself.
